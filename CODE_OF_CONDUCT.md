# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the project.

Examples of welcome behavior include:

- Giving clear, actionable feedback.
- Assuming good intent while still asking for clarification.
- Respecting different experience levels and communication styles.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, or publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that do not meet these standards. Repeated or severe violations may result in being blocked from participating in the project.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through the contact options on [github.com/doctorlai](https://github.com/doctorlai).
